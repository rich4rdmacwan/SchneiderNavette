Schneidernavette

A Navigation Application

What is it?

SchneiderNavette is a navigation application very much like the famous GoogleMaps. It is aimed to aid the user to explore a geographical region virtually, plan routes and journeys, "Find" places of interest and much more.

Why the name SchneiderNavette?

They developed a business in steel, railways, armaments, and shipbuilding. The name is a tribute to the excellent work done by them. "Navette" is the french word for shuttle. The project signifies the idea of exploring the city in a shuttle.
